Rosie the Riveter

Rosie the Riveter was a cultural icon that represented the working women of WWII. The most famous representation of her today is the famous “We Can Do It!” poster (shown above) created by J. Howard Miller. The purpose of this image was initially just to boost morale of women during the Second World War and it was not known by its now famous name, but now is a famous icon of the working women of WWII.
